Shipping Policy

We ship across Australia only. We do not currently offer international shipping.

Processing time

Orders placed before 2pm AEST on a business day are typically dispatched the same day. Orders placed after this cutoff or on weekends and public holidays will be dispatched on the next business day. During peak periods (sales, Black Friday, Christmas), processing may take up to 3 business days.

Delivery timeframes

We ship via Australia Post and trusted courier services. Estimated delivery times after dispatch:

  • Metro areas (Sydney, Melbourne, Brisbane, Adelaide, Perth): 2–5 business days
  • Regional areas: 4–8 business days
  • Remote / NT / WA outback: 7–14 business days

Shipping rates

  • Free standard shipping on all orders over $99 AUD
  • Orders under $99 AUD: flat rate $9.95 standard shipping
  • Express shipping: $14.95 (1–3 business days metro, 2–5 business days regional)

Final shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your address and selected service.

Order tracking

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can track your parcel using the link in that email or directly via the carrier's website (e.g., Australia Post tracking).

Delivery

A signature is generally not required. If you're not home, your parcel may be left in a safe location or taken to your local post office or courier depot for collection. You can specify safe-drop instructions at checkout where available.

Incorrect or incomplete addresses

Please double-check your shipping address at checkout. We cannot be held responsible for delays or lost parcels due to incorrect addresses provided by the customer. If a parcel is returned to us due to an incorrect address or non-collection, you will be responsible for re-shipping costs.

Lost or damaged parcels

If your parcel hasn't arrived within the estimated timeframe, please:

  1. Check the tracking information for updates
  2. Check with neighbours or your local post office
  3. Contact us at contactus@gopotty.au if it remains missing

We'll work with the carrier to locate your parcel or arrange a replacement / refund if it is confirmed lost. Damaged parcels should be reported within 7 days of delivery with photos — see our Refund Policy.

Public holidays

We do not dispatch on Australian public holidays. Expect minor delays around major holidays (Christmas, Easter, Labour Day, etc.).
