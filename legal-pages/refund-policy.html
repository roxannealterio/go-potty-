Your rights under Australian Consumer Law

Your rights under the Australian Consumer Law (ACL) are protected and cannot be excluded. Our goods come with guarantees that cannot be excluded under the ACL. You are entitled to:

  • A replacement or refund for a major failure (e.g., the product is unsafe, doesn't do what we said, or is significantly different from the description), and compensation for any other reasonably foreseeable loss or damage.
  • A repair, replacement, or refund if the goods fail to be of acceptable quality and the failure does not amount to a major failure.

This policy is in addition to your ACL rights and does not limit them in any way.

14-Day Change of Mind Returns

We stand behind GoPotty. If you change your mind about your purchase, you can return it within 14 days of delivery for a refund of the product price.

To qualify for a 14-day return:

  • The item must be unused, unwashed, and in its original condition
  • All original packaging, components, and accessories must be included
  • Proof of purchase (order number or receipt) must be provided
  • The return request must be initiated within 14 days of delivery

How to request a return

  1. Email contactus@gopotty.au within 14 days of delivery with your order number and the reason for return.
  2. We'll send you a Return Authorisation (RA) number and the return shipping address within 2 business days.
  3. Pack the item securely with all original components. Write the RA number clearly on the outside of the parcel.
  4. Ship the item back using a trackable shipping method within 7 days of receiving your RA number. We recommend keeping proof of postage.

Returns sent without an RA number may be delayed or refused.

Return shipping costs

  • Faulty or incorrect items: we cover the return shipping cost.
  • Change of mind (14-day return): the customer is responsible for return shipping costs.

Refund processing

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll email you to confirm:

  • Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.
  • Bank or card-issuer processing times may add a further 3–10 business days for the refund to appear on your statement.
  • Original shipping fees are non-refundable unless the return is due to our error or a faulty product.

Damaged or faulty items

If your order arrives damaged or you discover a manufacturing defect, contact us within 7 days of delivery at contactus@gopotty.au with:

  • Your order number
  • Clear photos or video showing the issue
  • A brief description of the problem

We'll arrange a replacement, repair, or refund at no cost to you, in line with the Australian Consumer Law. Your ACL rights are not limited to 14 days — they apply for a reasonable period based on the type of product.

Warranty claims

GoPotty products are covered by a 1-year limited manufacturer's warranty from the date of delivery. The warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal household use. It does not cover damage from misuse, neglect, accidents, unauthorised modifications, or normal wear and tear.

To make a warranty claim, email contactus@gopotty.au with proof of purchase and supporting photos/video.

Non-returnable items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned unless faulty:

  • Items that have been used and visibly soiled
  • Consumables (e.g., waste tank refills, cleaning sprays) that have been opened
  • Sale or clearance items marked "final sale"
  • Gift cards

Exchanges

We don't currently offer direct exchanges. To get a different item, please request a refund for your original order and place a new order for the item you'd prefer.

Cancellations

Orders can be cancelled for a full refund if they haven't yet been dispatched. Once your order has been processed and shipped, our standard returns policy applies. Email us immediately at contactus@gopotty.au if you need to cancel.
