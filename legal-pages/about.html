About GoPotty

We started GoPotty because pet parenting shouldn't mean constant cleanup.

If you live in an apartment, work long hours, or just want a cleaner home, you know the struggle: indoor accidents, soggy pee pads, the never-ending smell, the scrubbing. We tried every solution on the market and nothing actually solved the problem — until we built one ourselves.

The product

GoPotty is the world's first truly self-cleaning dog potty. When your dog goes, the liquid drains through a non-slip tray and is automatically suctioned into a sealed waste tank. Just rinse the tray with water and you're done. No pads, no mess, no smell.

Designed for modern pet parents

Compact enough for an apartment. Quiet enough not to scare your dog. Eco-friendly because it eliminates single-use pee pads. We tested it with hundreds of dogs and pet parents across Australia before we ever shipped a single unit.

Built to last

Every GoPotty comes with a 1-year warranty and a 30-day money-back guarantee. We use medical-grade silicone for the tray, ABS plastic for the base, and a powerful but silent suction motor rated for 5,000+ cycles. If anything goes wrong, we'll make it right.

Based in Australia

We're an Australian-owned business shipping out of Australia. Our customer support team is based here, replies in Australian business hours, and actually knows the product. No offshore call centres, no scripts.
